Overview

Welcome to the 20th edition of EuroSys!

EuroSys is a premier conference on various aspects of systems software research and development, including its ramifications for hardware and applications. Topics of interest for the conference include: operating systems, database systems, real-time systems, networked systems, storage systems, middleware, distributed, parallel, and embedded computing systems.

ACM EuroSys 2025 will take place in Rotterdam co-located with ACM ASPLOS 2025. Attendees will be able to attend both conferences through a single registration fee.

EuroSys 2025 will be a five-day event. The first day is devoted to workshops, to allow junior and senior researchers to interact in a friendly environment on cutting-edge ideas. The other three days are dedicated to the main conference track.

The 20th edition of EuroSys will follow the pattern established by the previous EuroSys conferences, by seeking high-quality papers on all aspects of computer systems.
